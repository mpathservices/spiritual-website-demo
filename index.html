Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reiki and how does it work?

Reiki is a Japanese healing technique that works with universal life energy. During a session, I act as a channel for this healing energy, which flows through my hands to you. It's completely non-invasive and works to restore balance in your body, mind, and spirit. Many people experience deep relaxation, reduced stress, and a sense of peace and well-being.

Do I need to believe in anything specific for these services to work?

Not at all! While an open mind is helpful, you don't need to hold any particular beliefs. Many of my clients come from various backgrounds and belief systems. The most important thing is that you feel comfortable and safe. I welcome people of all faiths and spiritual paths.

What should I expect in my first session?

We'll start with a brief conversation about what brings you here and what you're hoping to explore. I'll explain the process and answer any questions you have. Every session is tailored to your comfort level and needs. There's no pressure to share more than you're comfortable with, and you're in control throughout the entire experience.

How does past life regression work?

Past life regression uses gentle hypnosis to access memories that may be stored in your subconscious. You'll remain aware and in control throughout the process. It's a deeply relaxing experience, similar to daydreaming. Not everyone experiences vivid past life memories, and that's perfectly normal. Sometimes the insights come as feelings, symbols, or subtle knowing.

Are these services a replacement for medical or psychological treatment?

No, spiritual services are complementary to traditional medical and psychological care, not a replacement. I always encourage clients to maintain their relationships with healthcare providers. These services are designed to support your overall well-being and personal growth alongside any medical treatment you may be receiving.

How do I know which service is right for me?

Trust your intuition! If one service calls to you, that's often a good sign. You can also reach out for a brief consultation where we can discuss your goals and I can help you choose the most appropriate service. Many clients find that different services serve them at different times in their journey.
